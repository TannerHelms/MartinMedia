Our Roots

We are an Alaska born and raised company! Based out of Soldotna, Alaska, we spend our days discovering the breathtaking beauty of our incredible state and love to use photography and video to capture stunning scenery and special moments.

Whether it’s for a wedding, drone shots for your real estate and business, or documenting your hunt for Bigfoot (Yep! Been there, done that!), our team has the expertise and experience to bring your story to life. Our focus is always to create a finished product that will make you feel like you’re right back in the moment. (Or if you weren’t there, feel like you were!)

We have years of experience flying drones and all the needed licenses and certifications. One of our unique specialties is adding stunning aerial photos and videos to each of our projects. We use drones to capture stunning footage for Weddings, Realtors, Lodges, Fishing Guides, and more!
